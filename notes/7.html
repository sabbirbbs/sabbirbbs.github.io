Intro

That's great to protect your eyes & relax while using your computer a long.I figured out the technique online & thought to develop a script to implement the idea.20-20-20 rules is to move your eyes after every 20 minutes look at 20 feet far for 20 seconds.If you can move from the desk & look for something nature.That's why created a program in python.Which will alert me after every 20 minutes as default & i can customize the time by giving an argument in second.That's after 20 minute beep beep beep*3...

Source Code

from playsound import playsound
import time
import sys

print("Eye Care 20-20-20\n16.01.2021\n************\n")

try:
	timi = int(sys.argv[1])
except:
	timi = 1200

try:
	while True:
		print(f"waiting {timi} second.")
		time.sleep(timi)
		playsound("beep.mp3")
		playsound("beep.mp3")
		playsound("beep.mp3")
except KeyboardInterrupt:
	print("\rStoping the alert.")
	sys.stdout.flush()
